Dockside Changelog

Version: 1.9.33

New Features

  • Added an option in settings to enable or disable specific areas of the shelf (e.g., you can now disable the Notes area in the Files shelf and the Screenshots area, or the Downloads area in the Screenshots and Downloads shelf).

Fixes

  • Fixed a minor bug related to license management and added a shortcut in the menu bar to manage the currently active license.
  • Fixed a critical onboarding-related bug that prevented Dockside from detecting system events (e.g., Dock hide mode change, Space change, screen switch, etc.) on first launch.
  • Auto-detects menu bar customisation apps (like Ice, Bartender...), disables the menu bar info popups that may be misplaced if the menu bar icon is hidden.

Improvements

  • Hints and tips will show up when it is relevant to let you discover Dockside features.
