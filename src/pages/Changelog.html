Dockside Changelog

Version: 2.5.4

New Features

  • You can now make the shelfs show just your Apps! (like Dock).
  • Shelf now supports Smart Folders! Create your custom smart folders and map it to the area and see you saved search items right there on the shelf.

Fixes

  • [RN] I’ve added a subtle animation fix to ensure the hover remains centred on the anchor point instead of drifting diagonally. This creates a crisp file hover animation and is purely for my OCD ;)
  • Fixes clipboard history issue.
  • General improvements and bug fixes.
