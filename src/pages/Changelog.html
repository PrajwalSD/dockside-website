Dockside Changelog

Version: 2.8.0

New Features

  • Liquid Glass is now supported on Dockside shelves (finally!)
  • Introducing temporary drop shelves–use shake-to-drop gesture or hold shift modifier key when you drag to trigger them.
  • French language support
  • Added "Import from iPhone or iPad" continuity camera support.

Fixes

  • Liquid Glass in Dockside! Please try and explore, many changes and bug fixes related to shelf materials. There's a new Drop shelf (like Dropover!) for temporary shelves, drag and shake your mouse to trigger it!
  • General bug fixes
  • In single files shelf mode (Yoink mode), reducing height/width will keep the shelf centred
