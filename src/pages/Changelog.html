Dockside Changelog

Version: 2.1.0

New Features

  • Settings panel has visual elements now for easy understanding.
  • Introducing multiple drag areas in the Files shelf for better workspace organisation, plus swipe-able pages in the shelf for quicker access (only available in the second shelf–screenshots and downloads shelf)
  • Add support for up to 3 drag-and-drop areas in the Files shelf as opposed to one area, making it easier to organise and separate your projects or workspaces.

Fixes

  • General bug fixes and improvements to the core.
  • Autohiding is better handled in multi-display setup
  • Popovers and banners are now localised (in all languages supported). If you see any issues with translations, please feel free to drop a note via email or Discord.
  • You can drop into nested folders in the shelf with drop zones! just hover longer on the drop zone to see the nested folders.
