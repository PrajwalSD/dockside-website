Dockside Changelog

Version: 2.9.8

New Features

  • Add simple notes with drop shelf
  • New shelf manager (experimental!)
  • Bulk selection of items in the drop shelf is easier
  • Ability to toggle auto dismiss a drop shelf if the shelf becomes empty.
  • Added "Shelf Library" to easily see your saved shelves in one glance in Settings.

Fixes

  • Fixed a critical issue with full screen app detection and exclusion
  • You can now control text drops on drag in and drag out.
  • Fixed an issue with deletes
