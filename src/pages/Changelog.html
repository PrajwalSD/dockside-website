Dockside Changelog

Version: 2.5.2

New Features

  • The second shelf now lets you monitor multiple sources simultaneously! It's a big change that let's you view the latest or most recently changed files from screenshots, downloads, your favourite folders, reported changes by macOS in Recents and your Clipboard history all in one area if configured so!
  • Control area split in the shelf via right-click menu.
  • An exciting new way to browse/add your files, folders and apps right inside the shelf - click the "+" in Files shelf to find out!! :)

Fixes

  • Performance improvements
  • Fixed the green-dot (latest file) indicator discrepancy.
  • Subtle visual feedback on opening a file in shelf.
  • Updates to settings page design
  • Clipboard history (internal) folder management has been fixed.
